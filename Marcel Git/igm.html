What is a Serology / IGM Test?

Serology tests detect the presence of antibodies in the blood from the bodyâ€™s adaptive immune response to an infection, like COVID-19.

What is the difference between a Serology Test and PCR Swab Test?

Individuals can only be diagnosed with COVID-19 infection when they undergo PCR swab testing.

However, COVID-19 serology tests are unable to reveal whether a person is actively infected with the virus. Serology tests, which instead identify prior infections, are frequently used for contact tracing, international COVID-19 immunization verification, and pre-departure screening (if required by the destination country).
