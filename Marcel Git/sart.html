What is a Supervised ART Test?

A supervised ART test is a test conducted in your own home, under the supervision of our trained doctors, so that you do not have to leave the comforts of your home to find out whether you are covid positive. Conduct the test in the own confines of your home and receive creditation about the verity of your results as soon as 15 minutes. A viable option for anyone who is feeling unwell or needs verifcation from your supervisors or higher ups about your covid status.

When to get a Supervised ART?

If you are feeling unwell, you don’t need to travel to a Quick Test Centre (QTC) or clinic for testing. You can choose to get tested via a supervised self-swab Covid-19 ART (Tele-ART) over video consultation with Aurora Medical and receive the result within 2 hours.

Get a supervised self administered ART through video consultation wherever you are via Aurora Medical's mobile app. Supervised-ART is approved for Pre Departure Test (for suitable destination country), Returning residents to Singapore and before nightlife activities.


Who's suitable to do a Supervised ART?


Pre-Departure Testing
Applicable for on-arrival requirements of specific destination countries. Results valid for 24 hours; Check your destination country's test requirements.

Inbound Travellers
Applicable for select groups of travellers trying to travel to Singapore. Available for Singapore Residents, Permanent Residents, and Work Pass Holders entering or transiting Singapore.
