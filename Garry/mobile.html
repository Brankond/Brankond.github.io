What is a Mobile Departure Swab Test?

Travelling somewhere soon? We offer mobile departure swab tests for those who are unable to travel down to any one of our branches. Just make sure to book at least 20 hours in advance for our normal mobile pre-departure swab tests, and at least 5 hours in advance for our express mobile departure swab tests.

Note: This service is only applicable for addresses in mainland Singapore and excludes Sentosa. All tests will be conducted in one appointment and location. This service does not include serology test and is not suitable for those travelling to countries which require IgM or anti-bodies tests.

Test result will be available within 6 hours

(Mobile Departure Swab tests only available from 8am-9pm 7 days of the week.).
